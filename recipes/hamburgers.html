Hamburgers

Description

These tasty sandwiches will make your mouth water!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce (optional)
  • Tomato (optional)
  • Any other ingredients (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Warm up grill
    2. Make ground beef into balls and then flatten them into circular patties
    3. When grill is hot enough, place patties over direct heat
    4. After 2-3 min, flip the patties over
    5. After another 2-3 min, move patties over indirect heat
    6. Once the patties' internal temperatures reach 160 degrees Fahrenheit, remove from grill. Let the patties rest 2-5 minutes before eating
    7. Add any additional toppings (e.g. lettuce, tomatoes, cheese) if desired

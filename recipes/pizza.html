Pizza

Description

A "do-it-yourself" tasty Italian treat that you can dazzle your guests with!

Ingredients

  • Non-stick cooking spray
  • Slice of naan bread
  • Shredded mozarella cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Toppings (optional; for example, pepperonie, sausage, mushrooms, green peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Use non-stick cooking spray on a cooking pan and place naan bread on it
  3. Spread light layer of pizza sauce on top of bread
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese so it covers the pizza sauce
  5. Place any toppings on top of the cheese. The more you place on, the longer the cooking time will probably be
  6. Put pizza in the oven for 8-12 minutes. Pizza is done with the cheese on top starts to brown
  7. Remove pizza from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes. Then, cut the pizza into slices or squares and eat!

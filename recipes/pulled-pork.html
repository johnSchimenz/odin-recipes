Pulled Pork

Description

Make a ton of food for a large group of people with this filling sandwich creation!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Pork shoulder
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Water
  • Choice of rub/seasoning

Steps

  1. Cover every side of pork shoulder with rub/seasoning
  2. Place pork shoulder in a slow cooker
  3. Pour 0.25-0.5 cups of water into bottom of the slow cooker
  4. Cook the pork shoulder 5-8 hours on low (preferred) or 2-5 hours on high until internal temperature is at least 200 degrees Fahrenheit
  5. Remove pork shoulder from the slow cooker and shred it using 2 forks
  6. Put shredded pork back into slow cooker, mix in 1-2 cups of BBQ sauce, and allow the slow cooker to warm up the pulled pork
  7. Once pork is warmed up enough, serve on hamburger buns and dig in!
